ABF Discipleship – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

ABF Discipleship (“the App”) is a tool designed to help users participate in discipleship, group engagement, training progress, and spiritual growth. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information – Username, password, and user profile details provided during account creation.
  • Group & Discipleship Data – Group assignments, training progress, attendance, and activity logs.
  • Automatically Collected Data – Basic technical data such as device type, browser type, and standard logs.

The App does not collect payment information, biometric information, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to ABF Discipleship features
  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Track discipleship progress
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain system security
  • Communicate necessary updates

We do not sell or share your information with third-party advertisers.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted HTTPS connections, secure authentication, limited internal access, and encrypted storage when applicable. Although no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share data only when necessary to:

  • Provide the App’s functionality
  • Comply with lawful requests
  • Maintain operational security

Your information is never shared with marketers or advertisers.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Your Choices

Users may request correction or deletion of their personal information or removal from group rosters by contacting us.
